Productivity Mode Project

Jul 2021 - Nov 2021

Soon after I completed this project Apple released a new update with a function called Focus, however, it doesn't quite address all the issues I looked into for my version. I looked into the way that curiosity gets the better of peoples productivity. Even with notifications silenced the phone addiction doesn't disappear and I was determined to find a solution. Concept App

I created a concept app called OverRide that would minimise the time it took to interact on the phone by minimising the choices available. I broke up lengthy processes of searching through messages and emails into simple breakthrough contacts that show up right on the home screen. I took out the sensory overload by having the majority of the interface greyscale with only crucial messages in colour.
